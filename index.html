Programming is an adventure

I never thought that programming would fascinate me so much. Although I closed this chapter once, I return to it after a break and I rub my eyes with amazement.

The way programming technologies have developed in recent years has confirmed my belief that programming can be not only a way of self-improvement but also a great adventure.

Almost all of us had our favorite computer game. Playing it gave us a lot of joy. Let's multiply this joy by 10, these are more or less the proportions between playing a game and writing it.
